##   Tools:

### John the Ripper
Basic and easy to use hashcracker

	john --wordlist=FILE

use

	john --show passwordfile

to view cracked hashes

###hashcat
Better password cracker, uses GPU's

###crunch
generates wordlists

###cewl
builds wordlists from urls
